How to Play

  • Tap anywhere on the screen to make your fish swim upward
  • Avoid hitting coral rocks and sharks
  • Each obstacle you pass earns 1 point
  • Try to beat your high score!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I turn off sound?

Tap the gear icon on the main menu to access settings. You can toggle sound on or off from there.

How do I reset my high score?

Go to Settings (gear icon) and tap "Reset High Score" to start fresh.

Why are there ads?

Splashy Fish is free to play. Ads help support continued development and keep the game free for everyone.

The game is running slow. What should I do?

Try closing other apps running in the background and restart Splashy Fish.
