Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Splashy Fish. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device Identifiers: We collect device advertising identifiers (such as Apple's IDFA) for the purpose of serving personalized advertisements.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with advertisements shown in the app.

Locally Stored Information:

  • High Scores: Your game high scores are stored locally on your device only and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • Settings: Your app preferences (such as sound settings) are stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our app. AdMob may collect and use data for personalized advertising purposes. This includes:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Information about ad interactions

For more information about Google's data practices, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5 and later, we request your permission before tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites for advertising purposes. You can choose to allow or deny this tracking. If you deny tracking, you will still see ads, but they may be less relevant to you.

You can change your tracking preferences at any time in your device Settings under Privacy & Security > Tracking.

Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. All game data (high scores, settings) is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

Third-party advertising partners may retain data according to their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Splashy Fish is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The advertisements shown are configured to comply with applicable regulations regarding children's privacy.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Request information about data collected about you
  • Request deletion of your data

To exercise these rights or for any privacy-related questions, please contact us using the information below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
